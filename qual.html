Classification of Countries

In the Pie graph, almost half of the 52 countries with 48.1% are Developing. Followed by developed with 32.7%, and then least developing comes in last with 19.2%.

Women's Access to the Justice System

The pie chart illustrates the percentage of countries with access to the justice system (the percentages are rounded off). While the portion of countries with women that have full access are larger than the portion with limited access, there is around ⅖ of the sample that limit women’s access to the justice system.

Women's Universal Right to Vote

The bar graph illustrates the number of countries and women’s right to vote in them. 4 countries do not give women the right to vote, while 48 do. The x axis is the right to vote (yes or no) and the y axis is the number of countries.

Women's Property Rights

Here, we can visualize that more than 75% of countries allow women to have sole ownership (84.6%). Only 15% of the countries hinder women from having full property rights.
