Women's Civil Liberties

The Histogram for Women’s Civil Liberties is negatively skewed. We can interpret that in the majority of the countries, women are able to enjoy freedom of expression and their access to freedom of the press. However, as you can see, there is an outlier that has a positive skew. From this, we can say that there is still a portion of countries that still deny women’s civil liberties.

Women's Political Participation

The histogram for Women’s Political Participation is negatively skewed with few outliers. This means that women do participate in politics. Despite that, there still are a few others who barely participate or don't participate at all.
